# 📊 Amazon Sales Dashboard 2022–2023
 
An interactive **Power BI** dashboard built to analyze Amazon's sales performance across product categories, customer regions, and payment methods for the period **2022–2023**. The dashboard turns raw sales data into clear, visual insights — covering revenue trends, profitability, discounts, ratings, and order patterns — all explorable through dynamic filters and slicers.
 
This project was created purely as a **self-practice exercise** to sharpen real-world skills in data analysis, DAX, Power Query, and dashboard design.
 

---

## 📌 Overview

This dashboard was created for practice purposes — to improve hands-on skills in Power BI, DAX, and data visualization. It gives a complete view of Amazon's sales performance: revenue, profit, customer behavior, and product trends through interactive visuals and filters.

## 📈 Dashboard Features

- **Total Revenue by Month & Product Category** — Line chart showing monthly revenue trends across categories.
- **Total Profit by Product Category** — Bar chart comparing Beauty, Books, Fashion, Home & Kitchen, Electronics, and Sports.
- **Total Profit vs Avg Discount** — Combo chart analyzing the relationship between discounts and profit per category.
- **Total Orders by Payment Method** — Donut chart (Wallet, UPI, Debit Card, Cash on Delivery, Credit Card).
- **Customer Region Map** — Geographic distribution of customers (Asia, Europe, Middle East, North America).
- **Detailed Product Table** — Product-level breakdown with Revenue, Profit, Orders, Discount, and Rating.
